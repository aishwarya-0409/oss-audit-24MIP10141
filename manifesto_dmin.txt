----------------------------------------
     Open Source Manifesto — Linux
----------------------------------------
Date: 29 March 2026

Every time I use Linux, I am reminded that powerful systems like the Linux Kernel exist because people chose to share their work with the world.
For me, freedom in open source means Ownership — the ability to learn, modify, and build without limits.
Inspired by this philosophy, I aspire to create AI powered daily based app and contribute it back to the community.
Just like the Linux Kernel forms the foundation of modern computing, I believe knowledge should remain open, collaborative, and ever-evolving.
